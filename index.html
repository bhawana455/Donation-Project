About

RiseUp Foundation has stepped in to provide food to affected people in Assam from its kitchen in Guwahati.
The Foundation will provide meals twice a day to approximately 700 people of Athgaon region in Kamrup district.

Precautions

Stay informed about flooding conditions in your area. Don't try to walk through a flooded area. Avoid flooded areas if you are in a vehicle. A stalled car should be abandoned immediately. Evacuate a flooded area immediately if instructed to do so.
